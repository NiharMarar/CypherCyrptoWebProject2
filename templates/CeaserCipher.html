Caesar Cipher

This is a Caesar Cipher Encryption/Decryption Game!

This game has two options, Encrypt Messages, or Decrypt Messages.

A picture of the concept of the Caesar Cipher


A video about the Caesar Cipher


A picture of Julius Caesar himself



Here, you can encrypt or decrypt a message using the Caesar Cipher.

Here are the steps:

1. Take a message you want to encrypt or decrypt and put it into the first(top) textbox.

2. Enter in your "S" or shift pattern you would like to apply to your message into the second(bottom) textbox.

3. Lastly, select the encrypt or decrypt button and your new message will be below.

There are a couple rules:

1. DO NOT use special characters such as !@#$%^&*()_+, etc. You can only encrypt/decrypt uppercase/lowercase letters.

2. DO NOT press the wrong button when encrypting or decrypting. (EX: Don't press the decrypt button when you did not put in an encrypted message)

3. DO NOT make the message to long as yes you can do however long you want, but it would look much cleaner if it were shorter.
